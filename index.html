Call For Papers


Service computing is an advanced research direction of software engineering and distributed computing. It takes software services as the research objects and supports the building, running and management of Internet service systems. In recent years, the rapid development and application of artificial intelligence has significantly changed the style of our life, study and work. Along with this, a large number of new-style intelligent services have emerged, with features of complexity, diversity and crossover. This brings new opportunities and challenges for service computing. In this circumstance, how to utilize the research of artificial intelligence and big data to prompt the research of new theory, methods and applications of service computing has become a hot research point.

 

ISC 2021: International Conference on Intelligent Service Computing will be held in Shanghai, China, from March 27 to 28, 2021.

Welcome to submit your paper https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=isc2021, and make sure to use the IEEE template to submit your paper.

 

We have two choices for publishing papers. 1) The paper with the length less than 8 pages will be published to International Journal of Intelligent Internet of Things Computing (IJIITC), and 2) The paper with the length between 10-15 pages will be recommended to the SCI-indexed journal.
