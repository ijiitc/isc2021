﻿ MCWC2021: International Conference on Mobile Computing and Wireless Communication

Call For Papers


With the development of Internet and Communication, we have a smart city and convenient lifestyle. In the future, the mobile and wireless related application will the most powerful and immersive computing, including advanced AI and machine learning (ML) capabilities, augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), constant connectivity and 5G capabilities. However, in order to support for various applications that depends on this huge data generated from massively interconnected devices, there are still many hot research keys and points..

 

MCWC2021: International Conference on Mobile Computing and Wireless Communication, Shanghai, China, Sep 25 to 26, 2021.

 

Topics covered include

    - Key drivers and requirements for 5G/6G/XG
    - Quantum communications for 5G/6G/XG
    - Integrating space and terrestrial networks for 5G/6G/XG
    - Resource management for 5G/6G/XG networking
    - AI-enabled intelligent 5G/6G/XG networks
    - UAV networks and Security
    - The massive IoT networks
    - Integration of wireless information and energy transfer
    - Big data analytics and privacy-preserving techniques
    - Interoperability, standards, service provisioning
    - Mobile cloud data centers: storage, caching, retrieval, migration, resilience, availability
    - Mobile networking and protocols
    - Mobile computing as a service
    - Quality of service (QoS): reliability, robustness, throughput, latency, recovery-time, and scalability
    - Optimization with artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and deep learning (DL)
    - Mobile cloud services: model, discovery, provisioning, security, and marketplace
    - Mobile computing for context/location-aware services
    - Mobile Edge, Fog, and Cloud computing

Submission Instructions:

 

Welcome to submit your paper https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=mcwc2021, and make sure to use the IEEE template to submit your paper with the length between 10-15 pages.

 

All accepted papers will be recommended to publish at the SCI-indexed journal, such as WCMC, JWCN, and so on...
For rejected papers, we encourage them to revise and resubmit to International Journal of Intelligent Internet of Things Computing (IJIITC).
